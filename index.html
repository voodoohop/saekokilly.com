About Saeko

Japanese-born Saeko Okuchi, known as Saeko Killy, began her musical journey at age four with piano, later picking up guitar at sixteen. Through her parents' influence, she discovered jazz and Brazilian music, while Tokyo's techno parties introduced her to electronic dance music.

After moving to Berlin in 2018, she became part of Club Sameheads, establishing herself as both a DJ and live performer. Her music effortlessly combines elements of dub, post-punk, and krautrock with electronic beats, creating a unique contemporary leftfield club sound.
